Company Refund Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2024

General Refund Guidelines

All refund requests must be submitted within 30 days of purchase. Refunds will be processed within 5-7 business days after approval. The original payment method will be credited.

Eligible Items for Refund

Products must be in original condition with all packaging intact. Digital products are eligible for refund only if they have not been downloaded or accessed. Services can be refunded if cancelled at least 48 hours before the scheduled date.

Non-Refundable Items

Sale items marked as "Final Sale" are not eligible for refund. Custom or personalized products cannot be returned. Gift cards and promotional items are non-refundable.

How to Request a Refund

Contact our customer service team at support@company.com. Provide your order number and reason for refund. Include photos if the product is damaged or defective.

Shipping Costs

Original shipping charges are non-refundable unless the return is due to our error. Customers are responsible for return shipping costs. We recommend using tracked shipping for returns.

Damaged or Defective Items

If you receive a damaged or defective item, contact us immediately. We will provide a prepaid return label. Replacement or full refund will be issued upon inspection.

Exchanges

We accept exchanges for different sizes or colors within 30 days. Contact customer service to arrange an exchange. Exchange shipping is free for items over $50.
