Chapter 1: Getting Started

Welcome to our Technical Support Manual. This guide will help you troubleshoot common issues and provide excellent customer support.

Chapter 2: Common Issues

Password Reset
If a customer cannot access their account:
1. Verify their email address
2. Send password reset link
3. Confirm they received the email
4. If no email received, check spam folder
5. Manually reset if necessary

Login Problems
Check if the account is active and not suspended. Verify the customer is using the correct username. Clear browser cache and cookies. Try a different browser or device.

Payment Issues
Confirm payment method is valid and has sufficient funds. Check for any holds or blocks on the account. Verify billing address matches card details. Contact payment processor if transaction fails.

Chapter 3: Escalation Procedures

When to Escalate
Security concerns or suspected fraud. Technical issues beyond your expertise. Customer requests to speak with a manager. Billing disputes over $500.

How to Escalate
Document all troubleshooting steps taken. Summarize the issue clearly. Tag the ticket as "ESCALATED". Notify the senior support team via Slack.

Chapter 4: Best Practices

Always greet the customer professionally. Listen carefully to their concern. Ask clarifying questions. Provide step-by-step instructions. Follow up after resolution. Document everything in the ticket system.
